Manic Organic

BRIEF
A brand new healthy organic ‘green’ food retailer is opening a number of stores, and you are to create the branding, visu- al style and some packaging design for them. They sell mainly healthy juices and small snacks etc for take-away. They want to give the feeling and impression that they are a very “earth minded” progres- sive company that offers only healthy, organic and wholesome products.

PROCESS

  • Research
  • Brainstorming
  • Logo, Blanding and Package Design
SOFTWARE
  • Illustrator

Playing Card

BRIEF
You will have to design the whole set for cards for one suit, as well as the design for the back of the cards. So you will design the King, Queen, Jack, Ace, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 and the back of the card (14 designs in total). The suit you choose to do, hearts, diamonds, clubs or spades is up to you. It is important that your suit works as a set and belongs together.

PROCESS

  • Research
  • Brainstorming
  • Design
SOFTWARE
  • Illustrator

Light Bulb Design

BRIEF
You are to design a carton to package one single light bulb. You are to measure and illustrate the light bulb using Adobe Illustrator, applying gradients or gradient mesh to make the bulb appear 3D. Press-ready finished artwork is required, including bleed and di-cut outline. Print and assemble a full colour mock-up.

PROCESS

  • Research
  • Illustration of light bulb
  • Logo
  • Product name
  • Design
SOFTWARE
  • Illustrator
